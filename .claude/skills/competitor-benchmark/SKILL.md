# Competitor Benchmark Skill

Use the competitor-researcher subagent.

## Benchmark scope
Research and compare:
1. Direct Belgian competitors in:
   - photovoltaic installation
   - domestic batteries
   - EV charging installation
   - electrical compliance
   - roof cleaning / painting
2. Local SEO competitors in Wallonia, Brussels, Liège, Namur, Tongeren, Riemst
3. Best-in-class inspiration websites in energy or premium home services

## Analyze
- offer clarity
- homepage hierarchy
- trust signals
- founder credibility
- local SEO footprint
- service page quality
- city page strategy
- case studies
- reviews
- CTA structure
- speed and technical quality
- content depth
- FAQ strategy
- multilingual implementation if relevant

## Required output
- top 10 relevant competitors or references
- table of strengths and weaknesses
- recurring market clichés to avoid
- opportunities where Be'energies can clearly win
- final strategic recommendations for design, content, UX, trust, and SEO

## Important
We do not want generic benchmark notes.
We want concrete actions that directly improve the final site.
